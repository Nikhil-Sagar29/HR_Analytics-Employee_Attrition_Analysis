📌 Title
--------------------------------------------------

HR Analytics: Employee Attrition Analysis

📊 Objective
---------------------------------------------------

To analyze employee attrition and identify key factors influencing employee turnover, enabling data-driven decisions to improve workforce retention.

🧠 Problem Statement
---------------------------------------------------

High employee attrition impacts organizational performance and stability. This project aims to uncover patterns and drivers behind employee exits using HR data.

⚙️ Tools & Technologies
--------------------------
1. Power BI
2. DAX
3. Power Query
4. Excel
   
📁 Dataset Overview
-----------------------------

The dataset contains employee-level information across multiple dimensions:

🔹 Demographics
1. Gender
2. Marital Status
3. Education
4. Age
   
🔹 Job & Organizational Details:
1. Department
2. Job Role
3. Job Level
4. Years at Company
5. Years in Current Role
6. Years Since Last Promotion
7. Years with Current Manager
8. Total Working Years
9. Number of Companies Worked
    
🔹 Compensation

1. Monthly Income
2. Percent Salary Hike
   
🔹 Work Conditions

1. Business Travel
2. Distance from Home
3. Overtime

🔹 Satisfaction Metrics

1. Job Satisfaction
2. Relationship Satisfaction
3. Work-Life Balance
4. Job Involvement
5. Environment Satisfaction
   
📈 Key Analysis Areas
--------------------------------------------------

1. Department-wise attrition
2. Salary and compensation trends
3. Tenure and promotion impact
4. Work conditions (travel, overtime, commute)
5. Employee satisfaction and engagement
   
🔍 Key Insights
---------------------------------------------------

1. Higher attrition observed among early-tenure employees
2. Employees in lower salary bands show higher turnover
3. Lack of promotion and growth opportunities drives attrition
4. Frequent travel and overtime are linked to increased employee exits
5. Attrition varies across departments, with certain departments showing higher risk

💡 Recommendations
-----------------------------------------------------------

1. Improve salary growth for lower income groups
2. Provide timely promotions and clear career progression
3. Reduce workload imbalance and improve work-life balance
4. Focus on high-risk departments and employee segments
   
📊 Dashboard Features
--------------------------------------------------------------

1. Interactive multi-page Power BI dashboard
2. KPI tracking (Attrition Rate, Salary Trends, Tenure Metrics)
3. Drill-down analysis using filters and slicers
4. Insight-driven visual storytelling
   
🚀 Conclusion
-----------------------------------------------------------------

The analysis highlights that employee attrition is influenced by a combination of compensation, career growth, work conditions, and employee engagement. Addressing these factors can significantly improve retention and organizational performance.
